Game of Thrones

The Game of Thrones television series is an adaptation of George R. R. Martin's A Song of Ice and Fire , but is different in some ways. The series is produced by HBO based on the Song of Ice and Fire novels written by George R.R. Martin, debuting in April 2011. Created by David Benioff and D. B. Weiss for HBO. The show was both produced and filmed in Belfast and elsewhere in the United Kingdom. Filming locations also included Canada, Croatia, Iceland, Malta, Morocco, and Spain.
