A Song of Ice and Fire

A Song of Ice and Fire is a series of epic fantacy novels written by George R. R. Martin. He began the first volume of the series, A Game of Thrones, in 1991, and it was published in 1996. George made the start of an amazing trilogy, he has to this day published five out of a planned seven volumes. The fifth and most recent volume of the series, A Dance with Dragons, was published in 2011 and took Martin six years to write. The fanbase is paicently waining for the sixth novel, The Winds of Winter. A seventh novel Dream of Spring is planned.

A Song of Ice and Fire takes place on the fictional continets Westeros and Essos. By the fifth novel there are 31 characters, and three main sotries, a dynastic war among severam families for control of Westeros, the rising threat of the white walkers in the upper north of Westeros, and the ambition od Daenerys Targaryen, the mad king's daugher, who wants the Iron Throne of Westeros.

A Song of ice and Fire received praise for its diverse portrayl of women and religon, as well as its realism. An assortment of disparate and subjective points of view onfronts the reader, and succsess or suvival of the characters is never assured. The books hae sold over ninety million copies woldwide and translated into over forty six languages. The fourth and fifth volumes reached the top of the New York times Best Seller lists upon their releases. There are several prequel novveles, a TV series, a comic book adapation and several card, board, and video games.
