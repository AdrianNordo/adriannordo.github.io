House Targaryen

House Targaryen is the name of the oldest family in Weseros. Westeros is a content in the fantacy wold of A Song of Ice and Fire by George R. R. Martin In Westeros it is accustomed to have a family name and sigil, the family name and sigil is something to be proud of. The Targaryen sigil is a three-headed dragon breathing flames, red on black. The house words are "Fire and Blood". It eventually became the first royal house of the Seven Kingdoms, as House Targaryen of King's Landing.

House targaryen is the only family of dragonlords who survived the Doom of Valyria, the Targaryens left Valyria about twelve years before the Doom. They resided for multiple generations on the island of Dragonstone, until Aegon The Conqueror and his sisters (who were also his wifes), Visenya and Rhaenys, began the first of the Wars of Conquest in 2 BC "Before the Conquest"

During Aegon's Conquest, the Targaryens build the Aegonfort in the new capital King's Landing, but the Aegonfort didnt last long and was later torn down and the Red Keep Was built.
